Tips for Building Lasting Habits

Small steps lead to big changes over time

1. Start Small

Begin with 2-3 habits instead of trying to change everything at once. Master these before adding more.

2. Be Consistent

It's better to do a little every day than a lot once in a while. Consistency builds momentum.

3. Track Progress

Use this table to visualize your consistency. Seeing your progress is highly motivating.

4. Forgive Slip-ups

Missing one day doesn't ruin your progress. Just get back on track tomorrow.
