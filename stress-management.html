Why Stress Management Is Important

Chronic stress can affect your physical and mental health. Learning simple stress management techniques can improve focus, sleep quality, and overall happiness.

🌬️ Deep Breathing

Deep breathing helps reduce anxiety and slow down your heart rate. Practicing this daily can create a sense of calm.

  • Inhale slowly for 4 seconds
  • Hold your breath for 4 seconds
  • Exhale slowly for 4 seconds
  • Repeat for 5 minutes

😴 Improve Sleep Habits

Good sleep is essential for mental recovery. Create a calming bedtime routine to reduce stress.

  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
  • Keep your room dark and cool
  • Sleep 7-8 hours every night
  • Go to bed at the same time daily
