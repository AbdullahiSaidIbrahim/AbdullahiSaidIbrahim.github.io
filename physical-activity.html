Move Your Body Every Day

Simple physical activities can improve your strength, energy, and overall health.

Why Physical Activity Matters

Regular physical activity strengthens your heart, improves mood, boosts energy, and helps maintain a healthy weight.

🚶 Daily Movement

You don’t need a gym membership to stay active. Simple movements like walking, stretching, or bodyweight exercises can make a big difference.

  • Walk at least 30 minutes per day
  • Take stairs instead of elevators
  • Stretch in the morning or before bed
  • Stand and move every hour

💪 Strength & Flexibility

Building strength improves muscle tone and metabolism. Flexibility exercises reduce stiffness and prevent injury.

  • Bodyweight squats and push-ups
  • Plank for core strength
  • Stretch major muscle groups daily
  • Practice balance exercises
